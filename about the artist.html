Where did it all start?

Going to design akademia, studying art, design and media

Moving on. Years later after primary school was done and I had done a little bit of this and that, but didn't quite found my place yet, untill I found a privateschool in Odense that teached you art stuff, that's when i signed up and lived there one year on the school. There I studied art history, signed up for art classes, such as qroquis, where you paint after real live models doing quick sketches of them, before they switch position. I also got into photoshopping.

My Passion: Pictures & photoshop

My style is either classic portraits or abstract paintings. I do both. My works are all about the aestetics!

This is my first photoshop work art, I named "Ildsjael", in danish means a person who is very passionate of something that they work with. The idea to it is, that I took my own x-ray, so you can see through me, added some fire to my body, representing the fire burning in me, when I'm passionate about something. Instead of a heart, I placed a light bulp as my heart, because my ideas comes from there. In a collage I show of the layers, i made of it, showing the process of how it became "Ildsjael", the finished work, that you see on the next picture. I deleveloped a new passion for working with layers and photos and doing collage, while working on photoshop projects.

I studied three years at the academia, learning to design, working in design teams, how you make layouts, do research and rough sketches, leading to a finished product and presenting it, in a portfolio. you can look at some of my works here
